Is Google Ads or SEO Better for Plumbers?

By Deante Powell • February 2026 • 6 min read

Short answer: Google Ads is better for plumbers who need leads now. SEO is better for plumbers who want to reduce ad spend long-term. Most successful plumbing companies use both — Google Ads for immediate leads, SEO as a long-term investment.

Google Ads: Leads in 1-2 weeks, costs $80-$150 per lead, stops when you stop paying.
SEO: Leads in 3-6 months, costs less per lead over time, keeps working when you stop paying.

Google Ads vs SEO: Quick Comparison

Factor Google Ads SEO
Time to leads 1-2 weeks 3-6 months
Cost per lead $80-$150 $20-$40 (after ranking)
Monthly investment $1,000-$3,000+ $500-$1,500
When you stop paying Leads stop immediately Leads continue for months
Best for New businesses, scaling fast Established businesses, long-term

When Google Ads is Better for Plumbers

Choose Google Ads if:

  • You need leads this week. SEO takes months. Ads work in days.
  • You're a new plumbing business. No online presence? Ads get you visible immediately.
  • You're in a competitive market. In cities with 50+ plumbers, ads let you compete instantly.
  • You want to test services. Want to see if drain cleaning or water heater leads convert? Test with ads first.
  • You have budget to invest. $1,500-$3,000/month minimum for meaningful results.

Google Ads Costs for Plumbers

Typical plumber Google Ads metrics:

  • Cost per click: $20-$60 (depends on keyword and location)
  • Conversion rate: 5-10% (website visitors who become leads)
  • Cost per lead: $80-$150
  • Monthly spend: $1,000-$5,000
  • Expected leads: 15-50 per month

When SEO is Better for Plumbers

Choose SEO if:

  • You can wait 3-6 months for results. SEO is slow but compounds over time.
  • You want to reduce ad dependency. Once you rank, leads come without paying per click.
  • You're in a smaller market. Less competition means faster SEO results.
  • You want lower cost per lead long-term. After you rank, each lead costs much less.
  • You plan to be in business for years. SEO is an investment that pays off over time.

SEO Costs for Plumbers

Typical plumber SEO investment:

  • Monthly SEO service: $500-$1,500/month
  • Time to rank: 3-6 months for local keywords
  • Cost per lead (after ranking): $20-$40
  • Expected leads (once ranked): 20-50 per month

The Best Strategy: Google Ads + SEO Together

The most successful plumbing companies don't choose one or the other. They use both:

  1. Start with Google Ads — Get immediate leads and cash flow
  2. Invest in SEO simultaneously — Build your organic presence while ads run
  3. As SEO kicks in (month 4-6) — Reduce ad spend as organic leads increase
  4. Long-term — Use ads for scaling, rely on SEO for baseline leads

Example: A plumber spends $2,000/month on Google Ads and $1,000/month on SEO. After 6 months, SEO generates 30 leads/month. They reduce ads to $1,000/month, total cost drops but lead count stays the same.

What About Google Local Services Ads (LSAs)?

Google LSAs are actually better than regular Google Ads for most plumbers. They appear above regular ads and you only pay for actual leads, not clicks.

If you haven't tried LSAs yet, start there before regular Google Ads. Average cost per lead is $70-$110 compared to $80-$150 for regular ads.

The Bottom Line

Google Ads is better if you need leads in the next 30 days.

SEO is better if you're thinking 6+ months ahead and want lower costs long-term.

Both together is the best strategy for plumbers who want consistent, predictable growth.

Related Questions

How much should a plumber spend on Google Ads?

Most plumbers should spend $1,500-$3,000/month on Google Ads to get meaningful results. Below $1,000/month, you won't get enough data to optimize. Above $5,000/month, you need to ensure your close rate and average job value support the spend.

How long does SEO take for plumbers?

SEO typically takes 3-6 months to see significant results for plumbers. Local keywords (like "plumber in [city]") rank faster than broader terms. If you're in a small town with few competitors, you might see results in 2-3 months.

Can plumbers do SEO themselves?

Plumbers can do basic SEO themselves: claim Google Business Profile, get reviews, add city names to website pages. Advanced SEO (technical optimization, link building, content strategy) usually requires professional help to see real results.
