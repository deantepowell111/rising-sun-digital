How Do HVAC Companies Get More Leads?

By Deante Powell • February 2026 • 5 min read

Short answer: HVAC companies get more leads through Google Local Services Ads, local SEO, a fast website with clear calls-to-action, and automated follow-up systems. The best HVAC companies generate 20-50+ leads per month by combining these strategies.

The average HVAC company spends $1,000-$3,000/month on marketing and generates 15-40 qualified leads. Cost per lead typically ranges from $25-$75 depending on location and competition.

The 5 Best Lead Generation Strategies for HVAC Companies

1. Google Local Services Ads (LSAs)

Google Local Services Ads are the #1 lead source for most HVAC companies. They appear at the very top of Google search results, above regular ads.

Why LSAs work for HVAC:

  • You only pay for actual leads (calls or messages), not clicks
  • Google verifies your business, building instant trust
  • You appear for "HVAC near me" and emergency searches
  • Average cost per lead: $25-$50

2. Local SEO (Google Business Profile)

Your Google Business Profile is free and can generate 5-15 leads per month when optimized properly.

What to optimize:

  • Complete every field in your profile
  • Add photos of your team, trucks, and completed jobs
  • Get reviews consistently (aim for 50+ reviews)
  • Post weekly updates about your services
  • Respond to every review within 24 hours

3. A Website That Actually Converts

Most HVAC websites are brochures. They look nice but don't generate leads. A conversion-focused website includes:

  • Phone number visible in the header (click-to-call on mobile)
  • Contact form above the fold
  • Clear service areas listed
  • Reviews and trust badges visible
  • Fast load time (under 3 seconds)

A well-optimized HVAC website converts 3-5% of visitors into leads. If you're getting 1,000 visitors/month, that's 30-50 leads just from your website.

4. Google Ads (Pay-Per-Click)

Google Ads put you in front of people actively searching for HVAC services. Unlike LSAs, you pay per click, but you have more control over targeting.

Best keywords for HVAC:

  • "AC repair near me" — high intent, emergency
  • "HVAC installation [city]" — high value jobs
  • "Furnace repair" — seasonal, urgent
  • "Air conditioning service" — maintenance leads

Budget recommendation: Start with $1,000-$1,500/month to get meaningful data.

5. Automated Follow-Up Systems

50% of leads go to the first company that responds. An automated system ensures you never miss a lead.

What to automate:

  • Instant text response when a lead comes in
  • Email confirmation with next steps
  • Follow-up sequence if they don't book
  • Review request after completed jobs

How Much Should HVAC Companies Spend on Marketing?

Most successful HVAC companies spend 5-10% of revenue on marketing. Here's what that looks like:

  • $500K revenue: $25,000-$50,000/year ($2,000-$4,000/month)
  • $1M revenue: $50,000-$100,000/year ($4,000-$8,000/month)
  • $2M+ revenue: $100,000+/year ($8,000+/month)

If you're just starting, $1,000-$2,000/month is enough to test what works before scaling.

What Doesn't Work for HVAC Lead Generation

Save your money on these:

  • Shared lead services (HomeAdvisor, Angi): Low-quality leads, high competition
  • Print advertising: Hard to track, low ROI
  • Social media posts alone: Nobody searches Facebook for AC repair
  • Generic SEO agencies: They don't understand HVAC

The Bottom Line

HVAC companies get more leads by being visible when customers are actively searching (Google LSAs, SEO, Ads) and by responding faster than competitors (automation). The companies that combine these strategies generate consistent, predictable leads month after month.

Related Questions

How many leads should an HVAC company get per month?

A healthy HVAC company generates 20-50 leads per month from marketing. This varies by season, location, and ad spend. During peak seasons (summer AC, winter heating), you should see 50-100+ leads.

What is a good cost per lead for HVAC?

A good cost per lead for HVAC is $25-$75. Google LSAs typically cost $25-$50 per lead. Google Ads cost $50-$100 per lead. If you're paying more than $100 per lead consistently, your campaigns need optimization.

How do HVAC companies get their first customers?

New HVAC companies get first customers through Google Business Profile (free), asking for referrals, door hangers in neighborhoods, and Google Local Services Ads. Start with free/low-cost methods, then reinvest profits into paid advertising.
